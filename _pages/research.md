My research interests lie at the intersection of **energy systems**, **carbon management**, **well integrity**, and **techno-economic analysis**. I am especially interested in work that connects engineering, economic thinking, and real-world infrastructure challenges.

## Well Cement Sealing Project
I am involved in research related to well integrity and cement formulation for abandoned and orphan wells in Oklahoma. This work focuses on improving sealing performance and exploring practical, cost-effective solutions for long-term well plugging.

## Computational Catalysis and Energy Research
I am interested in computational and energy-focused research, especially projects that connect technical modeling with real-world impact in carbon management and sustainable energy systems.

## Energy Systems and Techno-Economic Analysis
I am interested in research that combines engineering with economics, especially where technical performance, cost, and infrastructure decisions interact.
